ABOUT ME

Hello, my name is Eli! I am from New York and am currently living in Boston, where I graduated from Northeastern University.

I have been interested in technology since high school, when I found out you can build your own computer. I discovered my passion for coding early on in college, which led me to pursue a combined undergraduate degree in Computer Engineering and Computer Science. I continued on to get an MS in Computer Engineering with a concentration in Hardware and Software for Machine Intelligence, of which I focused heavily on the software side.

I feel lucky to now have the tools and fundamental knowledge to work on any idea that pops into my head, but I am always looking to expand my horizons!
